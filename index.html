The Problem

Shrimp has 10x more impact on the environment than beef. Why? Mangrove Forests.

The dense tangle of prop roots that distinguish the mangroves allow them to handle the violent rise and fall of tides. They are very important as a strong, natural barrier against storm surges, which are getting increasingly threatening as the global climate changes with rising sea levels.

But mangrove forests not only protect the land from the ocean, they have a major role in the climate of our world. Mangroves are critical to fighting climate change. Mangroves are a blue carbon ecosystem (along with sea grasses and salt marshes). They can be up to 10x more efficient (than terrestrial ecosystem) at absorbing and storing carbon dioxide - the main greenhouse gas from burning of fossil fuels - in their roots long term.

But how does shrimp have anything to do with this? Mangroves are cut down to provide room for shrimp and fish farms, as well as construction for many other things. The destruction of these mangroves have a massive impact on the environment and climate since they can no longer absorb and store carbon dioxide. Along with this, pollution from fertilizers, pesticides, antibiotics, and other toxic chemicals can be carried through mangroves and other surrounding areas.

About


Who are we?

We are Nano Shrimp - a nanotechnology company working on eliminating all need for toxic chemicals in the shrimp industry. We use nanotechnology and nanoparticles to provide safe and eco-friendly prevention of disease in shrimp. We also use nanotechnology to replace all harmful and toxic (to the environment and to people eating) chemicals in shrimp farming. Using our environmentally friendly nanotechnology, we eliminate much of the enrvironmental footprint of the farms using it.

And we aren't the only ones developing nanotechnology to eliminate disease, check out a research paper on using silver nanoparticles to eliminate bacteria such as E. coli Image from the experiment Click here to read the paper


How we work

Our target demographic is companies that buy from shrimp farm. We understand that some shrimp farms may not have the best income (or internet). However, anyone can buy from us. If you're interested in our products, feel free to email us: nanoshrimpco@gmail.com

Our Environment

Our environment needs us now. The mangroves, a vital part of our environment and climate, are being cut down. Thankfully, there are restoration efforts in areas that need mangroves. We understand that nanotechnolovy may not have the most beneficial impact to the restoration of mangroves which is why we are giving a part of the profit Nano Shrimp earns to restoration efforts. We hope that through our efforts and those restoring mangroves will help our world. We are funding the Mangrove Action Project, an NGO that works with mangrove forest communities, fellow NGOs, researchers, and local governments to conserve and restore mangroves and related coastal ecosystems.
